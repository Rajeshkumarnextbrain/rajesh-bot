## Instructions

1. Resolve date using get_current_time

2. Fetch:
   - Today's attendance
   - Yesterday's attendance
   - Last week's same day

3. Compare:
   - Present vs absent changes
   - Attendance rate differences

4. Identify:
   - Improvement or decline
   - Unusual absence spikes

5. Generate insights:
   - Workforce trend
   - Attendance health
